Street Fighter V: Arcade Edition(PS4)

Street Fighter V: Arcade Edition is a 2.5D fighting game and the first major updated version of Street Fighter V. It cost $39.99 for the standard edition, and is a free update for those who already own the original version. It features all the original playable characters (including the twelve DLC fighters), an Arcade Mode (which was absent in the original installment), new modes such as the Extra Battle Mode, selectable V-Triggers, as well as new visuals to the game's design. Street Fighter V: Arcade Edition was released on January 16, 2018.
